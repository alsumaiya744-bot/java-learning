			  FISH SUPPLY CHAIN BUSINESS-DEPARTMENT FLOW
			  
			  
1. MARKETING

1. Identify seafood export companies and retailers who need fish supply
2. Contact potential buyers through phone or business network
3. Visit companies and fish markets to introduce the business
4. Explain fish availability, quality, and price
5. Maintain contact with regular customers
6. Collect customer requirements for bulk fish supply
7. Improve marketing through customer feedback


2. SALES

1. Receive fish order from export companies or retailers
2. Confirm quantity and fish type required
3. Confirm price with customer
4. Record the order details
5. Inform the operations team about the order
6. Prepare delivery schedule
7. Deliver fish to the customer
8. Confirm order completion


3. OPERATIONS

1. Identify fishermen or fish markets for purchasing fish
2. Purchase fish in bulk from fishermen
3. Transport fish to storage location
4. Store fish in ice boxes or cold storage
5. Sort fish according to type and quality
6. Check incoming customer orders
7. Pack fish with ice for delivery
8. Load fish into delivery vehicle
9. Delivery men transport fish to cities (Chennai, Tirchy, Coimbatore etc.)
10. Deliver fish to export companies or retailers


4. ACCOUNTS

1. Record daily fish purchase expenses
2. Record transportation and fuel expenses
3. Record worker wages, food expenses, and water cans
4. Record electricity and ice expenses
5. Generate invoice for customer orders
6. Record customer payments
7. Record daily sales in system
8. Update daily accounts


5. FINANCE

1. Collect sales records from accounts department
2. Collect daily expense records
3. Verify customer payments
4. Calculate total income
5. Calculate total expenses
6. Perform profit or loss analysis
7. Prepare financial reports
8. Plan budget for business operations

6. HR (Human Resource) – SME Style

1. Identify need for workers (drivers, delivery helpers, fish handlers)
2. Hire workers through local contacts or references
3. Assign job roles (driver, delivery man, fish handling worker)
4. Explain work process and delivery routes
5. Maintain worker attendance informally
6. Ensure workers handle fish properly and safely
7. Provide daily food or basic facilities for workers
8. Manage worker wages and payments


7. MANAGEMENT – SME Style

1. Plan daily fish purchasing and delivery schedule
2. Maintain relationship with fishermen suppliers
3. Maintain relationship with regular buyers/export companies
4. Monitor fish stock and delivery status
5. Check sales and daily expenses
6. Review profit or loss
7. Solve business problems (delivery delays, fish quality issues)
8. Decide improvements for business operations
